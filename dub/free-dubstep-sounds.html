How long can a sample be legally?

The duration for which a sample can be legally used varies depending on several factors including the jurisdiction, the nature of the sample (e.g., music, literary work, patent), and whether the use falls under fair use or requires explicit permission from the copyright holder. In some cases, very short samples may be used under the doctrine of fair use, especially if the use is transformative, non-commercial, or educational. However, there is no universal rule for the length of a sample that can be used without permission, and using any sample without proper licensing or falling within a legal exception can lead to copyright infringement claims. It's always best to seek legal advice or obtain permission from the copyright holder before using a sample.

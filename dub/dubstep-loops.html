What the f is dubstep?

Dubstep is a genre of electronic dance music that originated in South London in the late 1990s. It is characterized by its sparse, syncopated rhythmic patterns with prominent sub-bass frequencies, and the occasional use of reggae-like drum patterns. The genre gained significant popularity in the early to mid-2000s and has influenced many other genres of music. Dubstep is known for its heavy use of wobble bass, where an instrument's volume or pitch is manipulated, creating a distinctive sound.

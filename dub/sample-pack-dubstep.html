Can I resell a beat I bought?

Whether you can resell a beat you bought depends on the licensing agreement you entered into at the time of purchase. If you bought the beat with an exclusive license, you might have the right to resell it, but this is not common and should be clearly stated in the agreement. However, if you purchased a non-exclusive license, you typically cannot resell the beat, as the producer retains the rights to sell the beat to multiple buyers. Always review the terms of your license agreement carefully to understand your rights and limitations.

Do rappers need permission to sample songs?

This process involves clearing the sample, which can include negotiating terms and potentially paying for the rights to use the music.
